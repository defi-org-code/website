Decentralized alert nodes

Notification schemas are constantly executed by protocol alert nodes in order to analyze new blocks of on-chain data for notifying protocol subscribers. Alert nodes are currently supported on Orbs Network, a permissionless PoS blockchain, and executed by the public validators of the network.

Open source iOS and Android app

Users can receive notifications for free by using a sleek open-source iOS and Android app, or through Discord, Twitter, Telegram, etc.
